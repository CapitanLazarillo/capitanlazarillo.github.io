# Privacy Policy for Capitán Lazarillo

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Capitán Lazarillo ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the iOS application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
We believe in "Privacy by Design." Capitán Lazarillo is a utility app that operates locally on your device.

Location Data: The App requires access to your device's GPS location to calculate your heading and distance to safe harbors. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Bluetooth Data: The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with your haptic hardware. This connection is used solely for real-time haptic feedback. We do not store or track your Bluetooth device identifiers.

Personal Information: We do not require an account, email address, or any personal identification to use the App.

## 2. Data Storage
All navigation settings and device preferences are stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all associated data is removed.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.
